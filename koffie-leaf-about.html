Our Story

Koffie Leaf was started by two brothers Sam and Joe from South Africa. Sam and Joe are two baristas who love coffee and coffee art. Once they started working as baristas, they knew that they wanted to open one of their own. Their love and determination resulted in the creation of Koffie Leaf. There is currently only one Koffie Leaf which is located in San Francisco, California. Sam and Joe are always experimenteing with coffee art designs and coffee flavors to keep the menu fresh and exciting. And their new offers always keep customers wondering what will be on the menu next.

Koffie Leaf is a coffee and tea cafe where one can relax with their favorite drink. Order a breakfast meal or take a pastry to go with your drink of choice. Sam and Joe always work toward having great customer service and providing the best coffee, coffee art, tea and food. Be sure to come by, get coffee or tea and relax with a snack.
